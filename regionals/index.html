Huntington Robotics Regional Information


You must have completed the STIMS sign-up in order to be granted entry at competition! For more information, please follow the steps here, or contact Abby Holmes.


Hofstra


The SBPLI (Hofstra) Regional competition will take place from Thursday, March 30 through Saturday, April 1. Team members will be attending Thursday, Friday, and Saturday


The event will take place at Hofstra University's David S. Mack Sports Complex. The direct address can be found linked below:
Hofstra University Hofstra Arena Hempstead, NY 11550 USA


Permission slips for this regional are due by Wednesday, March 29th at the latest. Click below to access the permission slip for each day:

Thursday, March 30 Friday, March 31 Saturday, April 1

If you are taking the bus, please arrive at the high school no later than 7:00am each day. We will arrive back at the high school around 8:00pm every day.


All members must wear their team shirts to competition! If you would like to purchase a team shirt for $13, please contact Bella McGinniss


Lunch will not be provided! Students must either bring money to buy food at the event, or they must bring their own lunch.


If you will be at the competition but will not be taking the bus back to Huntington or will be leaving early, you must complete both the Transportation Release Form below and the Competition Record Form. If you are just arriving late you only have to complete the Competition Record Form. In addition to completing the necessary forms, you must submitt a note with the time(s) you expect to leave/arrive.

The note can be submitted to Mr. Reynolds, the team captain, or the team vice-captain. Transportation Record Form must be submitted to Mr. Reynolds, the team captain, or the team vice-captain by Wednesday, March 29th.
