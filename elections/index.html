Post-season Elections


Elections will take place on Thursday, June 8th, after school in the woodshop.


Positions being elected are captain, vice-captain, chief technology officer, treasurer, and head of marketing and public relations. If you are interested in running for a position, you must notify the team captain by 11:59pm on Sunday, June 4th, 2017. You must also have a letter of intent prepared by the end of the school day on Thursday, June 8th.


Information about election procedures can be found at the link below:
Election Procedures


Even if you are not running for a position, you may be selected to be a member of the "nomination committee." This committee meets on the day of elections and makes the final decision about which candidate is elected to a position, as outlined in the above document. If you are selected to be on the committee you must be available for the entirety of the election on Thursday, June 8th.
