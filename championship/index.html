Huntington Robotics World Championship Information


You must have completed the STIMS sign-up in order to be granted entry at competition! For more information, please follow the steps here, or contact Abby Holmes.


St. Louis


World championships will take place from Wednesday, April 26 through Saturday, April 29. However, due to time allocated for travel, students will be leaving on Tuesday, April 25 and returning on Sunday, April 30.


The event will take place at The Dome at the America's Center. The direct address can be found linked here: 901 N Broadway, St. Louis, MO 63101


The total price per student of this trip amounts to $210. The price per student covers 3 dinners, the baseball game (whether he/she decides to go to it or not), 3 team shirts, the bus ride, and the hotel cost. Students should bring some extra money for lunch expenses, dinner expenses for both Tuesday and Saturday, and other miscellaneous expenses.

The team will be on meeting at Huntington High School on Tuesday at 4:00pm to leave for St. Louis on the coach bus. In order to conserve space on the bus, students may only bring carry-ons and duffel bags. It is recommended that students bring a pillow, blanket, entertainment, and some schoolwork for the bus to and from St. Louis. Students should also bring their swimsuits since there is a pool at the hotel.


If you will be arriving at St. Louis separately, please notify Mr. Santiago at osantiago@hufsd.edu


All members must bring their team shirts to wear in St. Louis!


All students will be going to see the local professional baseball team, the St. Louis Cardinals, play at the famous Busch Stadium. The game will take place on Friday from approximately 7:00 to 10:00pm.
